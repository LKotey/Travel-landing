Here are some places where you can go

  • Larnaca

    Visit the famous Salt Lake, home to pink flamingos, and the medieval castle offering stunning sea views.

  • Limassol

    Explore the beautiful beaches, stroll through the old town with its narrow streets, and visit the ancient ruins in the Kourion Archaeological Park.

  • Omodos

    Enjoy the atmosphere of a traditional village, taste the finest Cypriot wines, and savor traditional dishes in local taverns.

  • Paphos

    Immerse yourself in history by visiting ancient mosaics and archaeological parks, which are part of the UNESCO World Heritage list.

  • Troodos

    Delight in the beauty of mountain landscapes and visit the famous Kykkos Monastery, one of the most significant on the island.

  • Akamas Peninsula

    For nature lovers, we offer a tour of this reserve with crystal-clear waters, exotic plants, and scenic trails.
